Goals

The goal of this website is firstly to inform and encourage people interested in Cosplay to join the CossiePlay events and purchase costumes from them.

Success of the website is based upon the increase in participation of events as well as any increases in sales following it's creation.

The target audience of this website is families. Cossieplay targets families by having costumes of all sizes and holding child friendly events.
by doing this, CossiePlay allows families to enjoy Cosplay together.
